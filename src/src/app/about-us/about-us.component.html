Peak Processing serves as the Senior Design Project for the following undergraduate students at the University of Wyoming. The objective of this site is to display the skills of each student in their respective field. For Adeline and Dylan, both of whom are web developers, this project serves as a basis to demonstrate front-end and back-end development while also allowing us to express ourselves creatively as individuals. For Austin, this project enables him to showcase his statistical background by creating the AI model that classifies mountains peaks and ranges.
