Summit Guide by Peak Processing serves as the Senior Design Project for the following undergraduate students at the University of Wyoming. The objective of this site is to display the skills of each student in their respective fields. For Adeline and Dylan, both of whom are web developers, this project serves as a basis to demonstrate front-end and back-end development while also allowing them to express themselves creatively. For Austin, this project enables him to showcase his statistical background by creating the machine learning model that classifies mountain peaks and ranges.


Adeline Reichert

B.S. Computer Science

Adeline's current interests lie in web development and she is enjoying exploring and constantly learning different pieces of the development stack. In her free time, she enjoys hosting board game nights and getting lost in all different types of movies.


Dylan Miller

Dylan is a web developer who is passionate about all aspects of computing. As a hardware engineer, he has specialized in the physical design of integrated circuits (VLSI) and higher-level digitial design (VHDL). As a web developer, he is passionate about both front-end and back-end development for modern web applications.
